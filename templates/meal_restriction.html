ABOUT DIETS

SPECIFIC DIETS

Some guest follow a specific diet such as meatless meals or gluten-free. Offer a meal that takes in account some diet restrictions allows you to touch a larger group of potential guests.

Allergens

An allergen is a substance that may trigger an allergic reaction following a contact, an ingestion or inhalation. A guest suffering from known allergens should avoid the consumption of dishes containing it.

Research filters

In order to minimise the risks of incompatibility due to those factors, hosts have the ability to point out the eventual specific diet that they will follow during the event, and guests can sort out meals with those criteria.
