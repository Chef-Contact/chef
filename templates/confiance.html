INSURANCE

Because your serenity is our priority, our insurance covers you up for free under the amount of 300 000€

What does the Homemeal insurance cover?

In the unfortunate and rare occasion where you would need it, the Homemeal insurance covers the events where the responsibility of the guests or the hosts is at take, within the framework of the links established between them by Homemeal.
You are protected all along your Homemeal experience under the amount of 300 000€.

What is not covered by the Homemeal insurance?

The Homemeal insurance cannot substitute itself to the civil liability of the host or the guest, included in every habitation insurance contracts. It comes to complete this one in the case of an incident. In order to obtain more information regarding the Homemeal insurance, please contact us at the following address: hello@affiniteam.fr.
If any trouble, send us an email at contact@homemeal.com, we will answer as soon as possible.

Who can I contact if any problem occurs?

Thanks to a partnership with Lloyd's, one of the greatest name in the world when it comes to insurance, a team is at your service if needed in order to answer all your questions and go through any troubles with you. <(contact@homemeal.com).

What are the countries where the Homemeal insurance applies?

The Homemeal insurance covers every country where hosts receive guests via the use of Homemeal
